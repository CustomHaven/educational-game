About Our Academy

Overthinkers Academy is a place where you can challenge your historical knowledge through fun quizzes.


How to Use Our Website

  • Register or log in to access the quizzes.
  • Navigate to the "Quizzes" section to start a quiz.
  • Select your subject and choose the difficulty level.
  • Answer 6 questions and submit the quiz.
  • View your results and get feedback.
  • If you don't pass, you can retry the quiz or study more using the provided links.
