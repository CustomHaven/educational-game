How to Use the Website

Welcome to Overthinkers Academy! Here's how to use our website:

  1. Register or log in to access quizzes.
  2. Choose a subject: History, Geography, or Languages.
  3. Select your difficulty level: Beginner, Medium, or High.
  4. Answer the quiz questions and submit your answers.
  5. View your results and try again or study more if needed.
